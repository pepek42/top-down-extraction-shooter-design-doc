# AI behaviour

Goal of AI is to create realistic, dynamic world. Ideally AI would be smart and resourceful. AI is expected to
* Use suppressive fire
* Use cover
* Use room clearing technics
* Use fire and maneuver
* Would fear for its life trying to retreat or even surrender
* Would relay info to other allies
* Could listen for footsteps/gun shots
* Would have limited vision - for example it will not be able to spot suppressed firearm shooting from longer distance from bushes
