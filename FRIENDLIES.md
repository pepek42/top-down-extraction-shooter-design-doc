# Friendlies

In raid player can have friendly soldiers

* [Player faction soldiers](#player-squad)
* [Ally faction soldiers](#other-player-organization-squads)

## Player organization

* Player can hire AI from his organization
* Player can align with any of the main factions in the end game
  * Choosing one faction will make you play their endgame missions
  * Will unlock ability to unlock tier 5

### Player squad

* Squad orders
* Single member orders

Type of orders
* Take cover
* Return to formation
* Defend
* Formation (line, wedge)

### Other player organization squads

Other squads can be on the map (infiltrating from the same spot or other). Player can give them high level order:

* Attack point of interest
* Aggressive recon
* Distraction attack
* Defend point of interest
* Fallback (to already taken point of interest or near infiltration/extraction zone)
* Extract

## Ally/neutral faction soldiers

* Ally/neutral faction soldiers may control part of the map or whole map
* They will not shoot at you
* They will not take any orders from you, they will just try to do their job
  * Attack
  * Defend
  * Patrol
* In many cases [corporations](./LORE.md#corporations) will be allied with you
