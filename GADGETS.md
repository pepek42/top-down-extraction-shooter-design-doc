# Gadgets

## Drones

Near future setting for sure would have quite some drones. They probably would not be as fun to deal with, possibly used only in small numbers to keep player on guard.

## Mines

Also, realistic but not as fun. Maybe used in small numbers with clear indication. Could be used by player defensively.

## Explosives

### Grenades

Fairly widely available

* Offensive - smaller explosive radius
* Defensive - large fragmentation radius
* Flash - disorientation/blindness effect
* Smokes - area denial/cover

### Heavy explosives

Less available, in smaller numbers, should be used wisely by player. Possible options:

* Grenade launchers
* RPG/LAW
* C4
