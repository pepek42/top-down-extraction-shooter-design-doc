# Loadout

## General loadout

* Depending on rig/belt character will have certain slots for equipment
* Character have slots as sum of their equipment (rig, pockets, backpack)
  * **OUT OF SCOPE** - backpack has separate slots and requires some time to interact with
* Magazines can store only one ammo type
  **OUT OF SCOPE** - mixing ammo types
