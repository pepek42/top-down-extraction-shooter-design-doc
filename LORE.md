# Lore

It is near feature. Poland has been partitioned once again! There are multiple factions fighting over it. You lead a small resistance group. Who will you align with?

## State of world

Whole world plundered into chaos as countries and ideologies clash once again. Poland is at the center of those struggles being torn apart by multiple factions.

Your resistance group operate in local environment that has been neglected by rivaling factions, bit is close enough to vital communication hub city. After gaining foothold if you want to expand

### Bandits

Outlaws that are creating they own "state" taking advantage of the current chaos created by warring factions.

* Side faction (non-main)
* Use mostly low tier equipment mixed in with mid-tier (bosses/VIPs)
* Mix of different weapons with more significant use of civilian equipment over captured military equipment
* Have several subgroups that can fight with one another

### Corporations

* Side faction (non-main)
* Does have security details contracting mercenaries/security firms
    * They can have from low tier to elit troops depending on what they protect/attack
* Can use other/exotic weapon systems
* Usually they are going to be on good terms with factions other than [bandits](#bandits) and [communists](#communists)
    * As their main goal is profit they can work with other factions but can also be hostile
